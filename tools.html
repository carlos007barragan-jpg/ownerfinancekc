Financing, Explained

How owner financing works

1
Your down payment

You reserve your home with a down payment. Each home lists its minimum — the more you put down, the lower your monthly payment.

2
Your contract

You sign an owner-finance agreement (contract for deed). Your price, rate, payment, and term are all in writing. We walk you through every line before you sign.

3
Your payments

One monthly payment that already includes taxes and insurance, on a 30-year amortization. No bank, no surprises.

4
Your home

Every payment builds your path to full ownership. You can pay ahead, and when you finish, the home is yours — with title.
